What is the Ideal Weight Calculator?

The Online Ideal Weight Calculator is an application that can calculate the Ideal Body Weight (IBW) of both men and women based on their height. Everyday, medical personnel in hospitals receive excessive number of patients and they need to determine basic statistics including IBW and it will take them a lot of time if they will compute it manually. Thus, this application can be very beneficial to the medical industry and even to anyone who can navigate it. It saves a lot of time and can give accurate results in just a few seconds. Through this app, healthcare professionals can now have a general idea about their patient instantly.

Different Formula For Calculating Ideal Body Weight

Our ideal body weight calculator uses four different formulas to find your ideal weight, as well as giving you your BMI . While the four formulas listed below give you only one number that you should be aiming for, BMI gives you a certain weight range you should be in.

For men, the ideal weight calculator uses the following equations:

  • Miller formula: 56.2 kg + 1.41 kg per every inch over 5 feet
  • Robinson formula: 52 kg + 1.9 kg per every inch over 5 feet
  • Devine formula: 50.0 kg + 2.3 kg per every inch over 5 feet
  • Hamwi formula: 48.0 kg + 2.7 kg per every inch over 5 feet

For women, the ideal weight calculator uses the following equations:

  • Miller formula: 53.1 kg + 1.36 kg per every inch over 5 feet
  • Robinson formula: 49 kg + 1.7 kg per every inch over 5 feet
  • Devine formula: 45.5 kg + 2.3 kg per every inch over 5 feet
  • Hamwi formula: 45.5 kg + 2.2 kg per every inch over 5 feet

Results:

Once you entered the above parameters, then hit the COMPUTE button, this Ideal Weight Calculator shows your Ideal Body Weight using the four fomulas of:

  • Miller formula (1983)
  • Robinson formula (1983)
  • Hamwi formula (1964)

Limitations:

Remember that the values found with our ideal body weight calculator are not one hundred percent valid for all. As long as you are in the BMI normal weight range, you are fine - don't go on a strict diet just to reach that specific number!

Also, our IBW calculator is valid for adults only. Don't use it to find the proper weight range of kids - consult a specialist instead. Also, don't use it if you are pregnant.
