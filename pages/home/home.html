What is Ideal Body Weight?

This is the most frequent question asked in this topic. The purpose of an Ideal Body Weight is to provide comparison and standard in determining whether an individual is healthy or not. It is a weight value computed based on different factors but mainly it is affected by one's gender and height. The Ideal Body Weight for men and women are computed using different formula.
